FAQ

Do you serve fika at Java Whiskers?

Yes, this is lika a traditional café where you can take a fika plus speend time with cats!

Way do you need to book in advance?

Java Whiskers have a limited number of slots/hour. To guarantee your visit, we always recommend that you book your slot on our website well in advance.

Where are the cats from?

The cats come from various cat shelters, emergency homes, organizations and private citizens. We then, together with our partners, select suitable cats.

Are the cats always avalible?

The cats can at any time retreat to their room where they have resting places, food and everything else they need. Their contact with our guests is therefore completely voluntary.
